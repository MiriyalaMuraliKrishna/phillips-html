Phillips Lytle LLP Receives MedTech Honors for Bio/Med Catalyst

Phillips Lytle LLP was honored with the Bio/Med Catalyst award by MedTech at the MedTech Honors ceremony held in Waterloo, NY on January 13. The award recognizes Phillips Lytle’s support for and commitment to the life sciences and advancing the interests of New York’s bio/med industry.
