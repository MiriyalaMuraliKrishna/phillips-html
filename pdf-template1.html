Phillips Lytle's diversity, equity and inclusion (DEI) efforts center around creating an inclusive work environment and recruiting, developing and promoting diverse attorneys and staff.

Our firm participates in national and minority bar association recruitment efforts; supports diversity scholarship programs and community engagement; consistently encourages attorneys to idea-share; uses equity impact analysis tools to ensure firm policies contribute to an inclusive and equitable culture; and provides robust training programs, responsive mentorship and competitive compensation packages.

As a firm, we continue to focus on taking into account diverse perspectives in our decision-making to maintain an equitable and inclusive workplace in which we deliver superior legal services and provide a fulfilling work experience for our attorneys and staff. Diversity, equity and inclusion are at the very core of our culture.

We also support the value that our clients place on diversity, equity and inclusion in their respective industries and are committed to collaborating with them to meaningfully make change. We predict that those opportunities will continue to expand, and that partnering with our clients in this way, and combining our energy, will have great impact in the days to come.
